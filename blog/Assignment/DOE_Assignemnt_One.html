Quesiton Three

What are the kinds of errors in physical experiments ? Why these errors are occurring? What is the difference between them?

Answer

Two kinds of error:

  • Systematic errors: may caused by the instrumental error, method errors, personal error, environmental error. These errors can be reduced or eliminated.
  • Random errors: is caused by the variables that cannot be controlled in the experiment, which can not be reduced. However, they usually follow the normal distribution.
